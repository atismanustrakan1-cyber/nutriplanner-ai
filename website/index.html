Overview

What NutriPlanner AI does and why we built it.

Our goal

NutriPlanner AI helps you plan and track your daily nutrition so you can hit your goals—whether you want to lose weight, maintain, or gain. Set your targets once, log what you eat, and see how your day stacks up with clear numbers and AI feedback.

What you can do

Set targets — Enter your weight and goal (loss, maintain, gain) to get a daily calorie target and macro split (protein, carbs, fat). Log meals — Add each meal’s calories and macros and watch your totals and remaining amounts update. Chat with AI — Ask how your meals look, compare intake to your targets, and get simple advice.

Who it’s for

Anyone who wants a straightforward way to track calories and macros without extra complexity. We keep the math simple and the focus on your daily log and progress. This is a planning and tracking tool, not medical or dietary advice.
