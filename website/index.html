Overview

An all-in-one nutrition management app built around your goals and constraints.

What NutriPlanner AI does

This project is an all-in-one nutrition app that supports:

  • Daily calorie intake — Track what you eat and see totals vs your targets.
  • Personalized meal schedules — Get AI-suggested meal plans based on your targets, what you have on hand, and your preferences.
  • Stock availability — Note what ingredients you have so the AI can suggest meals that use them.
  • Dietary restrictions — Set allergies, intolerances, or diets (e.g. vegetarian, low-sodium) so suggestions stay within your limits.
  • Financial limits — Set a weekly food budget so suggestions respect what you can spend.

Set targets

Enter your weight and goal (loss, maintain, gain) to get a daily calorie target and macro split. Log meals and watch your day stack up with clear numbers and AI feedback.

Log & plan

Add meals manually, search foods, or scan with your camera. In Settings, add dietary restrictions, a weekly budget, and what’s in your pantry—then ask the AI for meal suggestions that fit.

Who it’s for

Anyone who wants to manage nutrition in one place: track intake, stay within budget and diet rules, and use what they have. This is a planning and tracking tool, not medical or dietary advice.
